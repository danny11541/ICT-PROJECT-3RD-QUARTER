How to Prevent Fires


Unplug Items You're Not Using Always make sure to unplug appliances that are not in use as they can catch on fire.
Never Leave Your Kitchen Unattended Whilst Cooking Never be careless when there is a flame in your kitchen because accidents can always happen, especially if you're not there to supervise.
Keep Flammable Items Away From Heat Sometimes, safety in the house is just being organized. Keep your flammable items away from fires and electrical appliances.
Maintain your wires Faulty electrical wiring is a huge risk for home fires.
Keep a fire extinguisher around at all times Sometimes, after all of the precautions, it's inevitable. Always have a plan b for things, and thats why keep a fire extinguisher around at all times.
